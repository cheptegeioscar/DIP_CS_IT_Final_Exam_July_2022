quality process

Tororo cement ltd is committed to the manufacture and marketing of building materials namely cement, construction steel, wire products and galvanized iron sheets. Tororo cement ltd shall strive to satisfy the customers through quality products with effective quality management system.

Tororo cement ltd also ensures that the management and staff are continuously trained, are conversant with the requirements of the quality management system to ISO 9001:2015 and committed to comply with its requirements.
Tororo cement ltd aims to produce consistently and market its products which are cost effective and build the customer confidence by identifying and meeting their requirements so as to increase its market share. Tororo cement ltd continuously monitors quality performance and innovate improvement to address the needs of the customers and quality management system.

The quality policy provides Tororo cement ltd with a framework to establish and review quality objectives for business. This quality policy has been communicated to employees within the organization & the quality management system shall be reviewed continuously for its suitability and effectiveness.
