Tororo Cement started operations in 1996 after divesture and barely seven years later started Community development involvement. This is an indication that we placed community development among our priorities.

policy of social corporate responsiblity

The company plans and assists the community and stakeholders through a well defined Long term POLICY, and short term goals/targets. Currently the Company has a policy leaning towards the community particularly towards the Education and Health sector.
